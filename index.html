Maker Nexus is OPEN for members, classes, and camps

We are following the guidelines from the Santa Clara County Public Health Commission

There are no drop in visits allowed at this time. Book a workspace before entering the building.

Reserve your work area

COVID-19 guidelines

  • Be respectful and keep others safe. Stay at home if you don't feel well or have been exposed to someon who has tested postive to COVID-19.
  • Help us with contact tracing. Use your RFID card to enter the building.
  • Keep your face covered. Wear a face mask while in the building and parking lot.
  • Social distancing. Keep at least 6 feet from other people.
  • Clean your hands. Wash your hands or use hand sanitizer when you enter the building.
  • Disinfect your space. Clean and sanitize objects you touched after you are done with them.
  • Stick to your reserved space. Only use the space and workstation you have reserved
  • No food or drink. Do not consume food or drink inside the workspace.
