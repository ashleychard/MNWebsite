COVID-19 Procedures

  • Stay home if you don't feel well or have been exposed to someone who tested positive to COVID-19
  • Badge in with your RFID card (contract tracing). You will not be granted access without your RFID card
  • Wear a face mask at all times
  • Maintain a 6-foot disance from other individuals
  • Wash your hands or use hand sanitizer upon arrival
  • Clean and sanitize surfaces and objects you touched
  • Stay within your reserved area and workstation
  • No food or drink inside the workspace
