Donate

Your donations help us keep going

Financial donations help us pay rent, utilities, and staff salaries, as well as purchase equipment and supplies for the maker space. As Maker Nexus is a 501(c)3 tax-exempt organization, your donations are tax deductible.

Direct Donations

Give Online

One-time payments can be completed through Paypal, or by check. Use the button below to donate online. Please note that we pay a 3% transaction fee on credit card transactions.

Give Online

Give via Check

Checks give us the full amount of your donation. Make your check payable to Maker Nexus, and mail them to:

Maker Nexus
234 East Caribbean Drive
Sunnyvale, CA 94089

Monthly Donations

Monthly donations help us manage our cash flow.

Email info@makernexus.org to set up a monthly donation.

Amazon Smile

Amazon Smile is the easiest way for you to donate money. Select Maker Nexus as your charity of choice and then shop at smile.amazon.com as normal. Amazon donates 0.5% of the total price of your purchases to Maker Nexus!

Email info@makernexus.org to set up a monthly donation.

Scholarships

Sponsor a deserving member of the community to take classes, become a member, or fund an educator

Email info@makernexus.org to discuss sponsoring a scholarship.

Employer Matching

Employer matching helps us out immensely. Employers will contribute some money for every dollar you donate. We are currently on Benevity, TechSoup, and Guidestar. Please let us know at info@makernexus.org
