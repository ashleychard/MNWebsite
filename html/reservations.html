Reserve a workstation

During COVID-19, you must have a reservation to use equipment before entering Maker Nexus.

Members can reserve a machine for 2 hours a day, 6 hours a week. If the machine is not reserved by someone else, you can work more hours on a machine but need to yield to another member who has not used up their time. If you need a particular piece of equimpent in excess of these times, we may ask you to buy a more expensive membership. Our objective is to make sure we equitably share the machines between all members.

Each Monday we are open ONLY to members who are at-risk during the coronavirus epidemic. At-risk members only can reserve a 40'x40' portion of the makerspace for their sole use on Mondays from 10 AM to 5 PM.
