BOSS Classes

Our Basic Operations and Safety Standards training classes teach you how to use the equipment in our workshop safely, and do not require previous knowledge to take. After completing the BOSS class for a specific tool, you are then able to use the tool on your own with your membership.

Don't see the class you are looking for? Post a request to the appropriate Slack channel or email training@makernexus.org.

Woodshop I: Tools for Wood Preparation

Date: September 25,2020

Instructor: Eric Cavazos-Smith

Learn basic woodworking techniques and gain familiarity with Jointer, Planer, Table Saw, and Sliding Compound Miter Saw. Emphasis on fundamental techniques to take rough lumber to square and straight for use in other projects. This satisfies the requirements for using Maker Nexus' woodshop equipment that is covered by this BOSS class.

Cost: $60

Project Classes

These single-day classes give kids and teens great hands-on experience

Sew an Apron

Date: October 10, 2020

Instructor: Sarah Kramer

Learn to sew by making an apron! We will customize the designs, cut out our pieces, learn to serge the edges, add pockets, and finish our pieces. Participants will also learn the workings of a sewing machine, including winding bobbins and threading the machine.

You will learn how to:

  • interpret a pattern
  • select and layout materials
  • cut, iron, and pin your pieces
  • thread a sewing machine and wind a bobbin
  • perform basic stitches with a sewing machine
  • finish edges and seams
All tools and materials will be provided. We have plenty of patterns, however, participants are welcome to bring their own natural fiber (cotton preferred) fabric.

Cost: $90
