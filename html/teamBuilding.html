TeamBuilding Events

Have a group? Want to do something a little different than the usual teambuilding activities? We offer a wide range of activities that can be customized to your team and the amount of time you want to spend with us. We are happy to arrange catering for longer events.

Contact us at events@makernexus.org for more details.

Make For Good - Woodworking

Have fun while learning woodworking skills and collaborating with your team members to build custom furniture

  • Workshop: wood working
  • Duration: 3 - 4 hours
  • Team size: 10 - 15
Maker Nexus is partnering with local non-profits to design, fabricate and donate much needed items to their cause.

Your team will spend 3-4 hours together in our shop making furniture which is needed and will be donated to the Bay Area Furniture Bank (BAFB), the Guadalupe River Park Conservancy, Little Free Library, and other local non-profits in need of custom items.

Maker Nexus' expert woodworkers design the pieces needed and will teach and guide your team to fabricate the items. We provide the material and the tools and machines to mill and assemble the pieces. Your team just has to come ready to have fun and build for good.

The event is set up for a team of 8 - 15 people. However, we can accommodate teams of up to around 20.

Laser Etch a Glass

Have fun designing and creating a custom wine or pint glass.

  • Workshop: Design & laser etching
  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Team size: about 5 people
Join us with your team and experience a taste of custom designing and fabricating your own bespoke set of glasses.

Chose from wine, beer or cocktail glasses: create a vector file of your favorite logo, text or graphics and program the laser cutter to etch your unique design onto the glass(es).

Our instructors will guide you every step of the way and ensure that everyone will be a successful Designer and will have fun doing it.

For larger teams feel free to combine with activities like Design & Fabricate a Custom T-shirt or Tote Bag

Customize a Tshirt or Tote Bag

Have fun designing and creating your own bespoke T-shirt or Tote Bag

  • Workshop: Vinyl cut/ Heat press
  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Team size: about 5 people
Your team will have fun experiencing custom designing and fabricating their own favorite design, quote, image on a T-shirt or Tote Bag.

Create a vector file of your favorite logo, text or graphics and program the vinyl cutter to score the material.

Our instructors will guide you every step of the way and ensure that everyone will be a successful Designer, create a new favorite T-shirt and will have fun doing it.

For larger teams feel free to combine with activities like Laser Etch a Custom Glass

Amazing Resin Art

Play with colors, shapes and resin - be imaginative, spontaneous and enjoy the positive energy of creativity.

  • Workshop: Geode crafting with resin
  • Duration: 2-3 hours
Pour your inhibitions away with resin geode building in this hands-on workshop. Participants will dive into designing their unique crystalline structure using an array of India inks, acrylic paints, crinkle materials, textured beads, and ✩ sparkles galore ✩!

Make it subtle or make it pop, either way, it’ll rock!

  • Workshop: Create a logo with vibrant customized resin colors
  • Duration: 3-4 hours
Explore vibrant customized resin colors, in teams or each person can create a personal masterpiece, using sparkly crystals, strikingly colored inks, paints and a variety of techniques your instructor will share with you.
