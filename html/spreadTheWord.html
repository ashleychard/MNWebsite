Spread the Word

Shout it from the Rooftops

Word of mouth is an incredibly powerful tool. You like us, so tell others about us, too. Post flyers at work and on public message boards. Write about us on social media. Tag us in your posts.

Nextdoor.com is one of the leading ways people discover us. Feel free to share events and classes you are excited about on your community's group to help expand our reach.

Social Media

Make something cool at Maker Nexus? Learn something neat while you were in the shop today? Make a post, tag your location as Maker Nexus (if you are comfortable with that) and tag us @makerNexus, and add #makerNexus to your posts.
