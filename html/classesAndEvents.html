Adult Classes

BOSS Classes

Our Basic Operations and Safety Standards training classes teach you how to use the equipment in our workshop safely, and do not require previous knowledge to take. After completing the BOSS class for a specific tool, you are then able to use the tool on your own with your membership.

Project Classes

Our project classes teach you skills and walk you through making things. When you are done, take your project home and show it off to everyone!

You do not need to be a Maker Nexus member to take our project classes.

Skill Classes

Keep developing your skills in a particular area. These classes will help you grow and develop as a maker.
